## How to Play

1. **Click the Cookie**: Click on the cookie image to increase your score
2. **Unlock Features**: 
   - After 5 clicks: Restart button appears
   - After 25 clicks: 2x multiplier button appears
3. **Use Power-ups**:
   - Click "Restart" to reset your score
   - Click "2x" to double your current score

## Game Mechanics

- Start with a score of 0
- Each cookie click increases your score by 1
- Background changes to pink after 5 clicks
- Restart button appears after 5 clicks
- 2x multiplier button appears after 25 clicks
